# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students (ages 10-35+) who want personalized, self-paced learning. They're curious but may feel traditional education doesn't adapt to them. Primary context: using the platform alone, at home or in a cafe, usually on a laptop. They want conversational guidance, not rigid courses.

## Product Purpose

LearnMosaic is an AI-powered personalized learning platform targeting SDG 4 (Quality Education). It creates custom curricula, tracks progress via dynamic checklists, and delivers conversational tutoring — all tailored to each student's interests, goals, and timeframe. Success means the student completes their learning goal and feels the journey was built for them.

## Brand Personality

Warm, intelligent, encouraging. Like a brilliant professor who's also a friend. Three words: empathetic, precise, playful.

## Anti-references

- Generic e-learning dashboards (Udemy, Coursera) — too corporate, templated
- "Gamified" apps with obnoxious badges and leaderboards
- Cold, clinical SaaS dashboards (dark sidebar + data tables)
- Google Classroom — functional but devoid of delight

## Design Principles

1. **Conversation first** — The chat is the primary interface. Everything else (checklist, curriculum) is a supporting side panel, never the main event.
2. **Celebrate progress** — Every milestone matters. The end-credits ceremony should feel earned, not tacked on.
3. **Adaptive personality** — The UI tone adjusts per theme preset, but always stays warm and human. No robotic defaults.
4. **Disappear when not needed** — UI chrome fades into glass/transparency. The content and the AI are the stars.
5. **Safe to explore** — Delete, undo, and confirm actions are always available. Students should never fear clicking something irreversible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA target
- Reduced motion supported via `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Color contrast ≥4.5:1 for body text across all theme presets
- All interactive elements keyboard-accessible
