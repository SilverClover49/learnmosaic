The Problem

Learning is broken for most students

01

One-size-fits-all

Traditional education delivers the same content to everyone, ignoring individual learning styles, pace, and interests.

02

No progress visibility

Students can't see how far they've come or what milestones they've achieved, leading to disengagement.

03

Resource overwhelm

Too many courses, too little guidance. Students don't know where to start or what to learn next.
